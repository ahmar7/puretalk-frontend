RUTH (Really Understanding The Humans)


RUTH (Really Understanding The Humans) is a set of proprietary natural language tools that can generate, understand, or classify the intent of human language. RUTH is used both as a pre-processing element to add contextual awareness to our award winning TTS+, and to create human like conversations without scope for Conversational AI.
